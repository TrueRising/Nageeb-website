Our Story

From a one‑person studio to a trusted partner for businesses across Egypt, here’s how Nageeb came to be.

Where It All Began

Nageeb was born out of a love for crafting digital experiences. Our founder, a self‑taught developer and designer, began building websites for friends and local businesses over a decade ago. Seeing how a well‑designed website could transform a company’s presence sparked a mission to help more small and medium enterprises embrace the web.

Growth Through Collaboration

As demand grew, so did our team. Today, Nageeb brings together specialists in design, development, marketing and strategy. We’ve worked with retailers, service providers and startups, always tailoring our approach to each brand’s unique needs.

Our Mission Today

We believe a great website is more than code and pixels—it’s a vehicle for your business goals. Our mission is to build sites that not only look beautiful but convert visitors into loyal customers. Integrity, transparency and continuous learning guide every project we undertake.

Looking Ahead

The digital landscape evolves quickly. We’re committed to staying ahead, adopting new technologies and best practices so you can stay competitive. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex e‑commerce platform, we’re excited to help you succeed.
